Our Story

Ahl Al-Yemen... Your Family

Ahl Al-Yemen Restaurant is your first destination for authentic Yemeni cuisine in the heart of Egypt. We serve the finest Mandi, grills and stews prepared with the best ingredients and the most authentic Yemeni spices.

The restaurant was born from a genuine passion for authentic Yemeni cuisine and a sincere desire to bring the flavors of Yemen to every Egyptian family's table. Our team of specialized chefs ensures an unforgettable experience with every visit.

Whether you're looking for a warm family meal or a unique dining experience, we welcome you to a restaurant that brings you together with your people.
