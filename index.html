About Us

Ahl Al-Yemen... Your Family

Ahl Al-Yemen Restaurant is your first destination for authentic Yemeni cuisine in the heart of Egypt. We serve the finest Mandi, grills and stews prepared daily with the best ingredients and the most authentic Yemeni spices.

Whether you're looking for a warm family meal or an unforgettable dining experience, we welcome you to a restaurant that brings you together with your people.

Authentic Charcoal Mandi
Lamb and chicken slow-cooked with special Yemeni spices over natural charcoal
Professional Chefs
A team specialized in authentic Yemeni cuisine with years of experience
Fast Home Delivery
Delivery available — order now via WhatsApp with ease
